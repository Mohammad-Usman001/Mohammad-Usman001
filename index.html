Welcome to Your Urban Retreat at The Dolls House

Hotels & Resorts

25 Years Experience

Overview

Welcome to our charming industrial-style retreat, nestled in the historic and vibrant Jewellery Quarter of Birmingham. Perfect for couples seeking a romantic getaway, our unique home combines contemporary comfort with urban charm, offering you an unforgettable experience.

The Space

The Dolls House is a beautifully designed home boasting a stunning blend of original industrial features and modern amenities. The space is thoughtfully curated to offer a cozy and intimate atmosphere, ideal for couples.

  • Living Area :Relax in our spacious living room, featuring exposed brick walls, high ceilings, and large windows that flood the space with natural light. Snuggle up on the plush sofa, open fire place with your favourite book or enjoy a romantic movie night on our flat-screenTV.
  • Kitchen : Cook a delicious meal together in the fully equipped kitchen, complete with high-end appliances, a rustic dining table, and all the essentials you need for a perfect evening in.
  • Bedroom : Retreat to the romantic master droom, adorned with luxurious linens and soft lighting, ensuring a restful night's sleep with electric blinds.
  • Bathroom : Unwind in the elegant bathroom, featuring a spacious shower and indulgent toiletries. It's your personal sanctuary after a day exploring the city.

The Neighborhood

Located in the iconic Jewellery Quarter, our home is surrounded by a rich tapestry of history and culture. Wander through charming streets filled with boutique shops, cozy cafes, and renowned restaurants. Discover local artisan jewelers, explore the nearby museums, or simply enjoy a leisurely stroll hand-in-hand.

  • Dining : Savor culinary delights at some of the finest restaurants just a stone's throw away. From intimate candlelit dinners to casual bistro bites, there's something to satisfy every palate.
  • Entertainment : Experience the vibrant nightlife, with trendy bars and live music venues offering everything from jazz to contemporary beats.
  • Attractions : Visit the nearby museums, art galleries, and parks for a taste of the local culture. The city center is just a short walk away, offering even more to explore.
